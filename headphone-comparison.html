Who should buy which

Choose the Stellia if you want detail-first presentation, run off portable gear, listen to well-recorded acoustic or vocal material, and aren't treble sensitive. Best "wow factor" on first listen.

Choose the Vérité Closed if you prioritize long-session comfort, musical engagement over analytical precision, and listen across a wide range of genres including rock, metal, and electronic. Most forgiving of the three.

Choose the Stealth if you want reference-grade neutrality, the best isolation available, and are using it for critical listening, mixing, or travel. The "correct" choice if you trust measurements.

All three are within a stone's throw of each other technically — the choice comes down to tuning preference and use case, not which one is "better."

Figures for weight, impedance, sensitivity, and price are drawn from memory rather than a live data source. Sonic character ratings are subjective consensus distillations, not measurements. Confidence is high on the directional comparisons (Stellia brighter than Vérité, Stealth most neutral, etc.) and medium on exact numbers.
